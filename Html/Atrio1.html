Joseph, Tarda, Alp


Before heading out to meet with Tarda and Alp, Joseph went out to his garden to feed his dogs. He had 4 dogs named, Cinnamon, Chuppy, Steak, and Demonbloodhound. Cinnamon and Chuppy are siblings and are both small sized and brown to orange color, steak is ironically a cream color and is the biggest of them all. Demonbloodhound is a white Bulldog. Joseph had 4 bowls in his hand respectively with varying foods depending on the dog. Demonbloodhound started jumping excitedly while the others awaited their meal with respect.

Joseph: I know you want to eat first Demmy but I must follow my order of feeding so step aside.

The first bowl contained a mix of seafood, it was Cinnamons favorite.

Joseph: Okay Cinnamon, here you go. Be a good boy while im gone.

Second bowl contained pork, Chuppys favorite

Joseph: Chuppy, look over everyone else and make sure they don't get into trouble okay?

Third bowl was chicken wings, steaks.... favorite....

Joseph: Don't let them eat you all right doggy, youre the biggest so you have to hold back when playfighting, don't forget

And the fourth bowl, it had meat broth soup, because Demonbloodhounds bites arent strong enough that he has trouble with solid food.

Joseph: Don't forget demmy no bullying the others while im gone. Be nice.

It's not that the dogs were scared of Demonbloodhound, they just went along with it to make him feel strong.
